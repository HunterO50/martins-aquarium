Martin's Daily Tank Maintenance Tips

  1. Inspect the tank
    2. A tank’s water, fish, plants, and accessories should be checked daily for signs of disease, wear, color changes, or changes in behavior.
  2. Check water temperature.
    3. Water temperature must remain consistent to prevent illness in fish, especially in saltwater aquariums.
  3. Top off the tank’s water.
    4. Water evaporates from a tank on a regular basis, meaning this vaporized water must be replaced
